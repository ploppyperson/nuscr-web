Honorary President

L-R: Dee F Smith, Andrew R Wignell, Roger J Lawson

Honorary presidents are elected every 3 years at the annual NUSCR dinner. Nominations are held in November, before voting can take place when the dinner invitations are sent. The person with the highest number of votes gets announced at the dinner and they become the honorary president. A president can only stand for 2 terms, which means a maximum of 6 years.
