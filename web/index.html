The home of student bell-ringing in Nottingham

Founded in 1958, the Nottingham University Society of Change Ringers (NUSCR for short), is the student-run body for promoting campanology within the University of Nottingham.

What do we do?

A

We practice every Tuesday at 7:00pm in term time. This is in hope to make it for Sunday at 10am.

A

Be social. We host socials, sometimes even including handbells. We also host a formal dinner once per year, with past and current students. It even includes a ceilidh.

A

We do not ring the bells in the Trent Building, these are automated.

A

Go to the pub, and drink measurable amounts of alcohol. No pressure if you don't!

A

Go on tour! In summer, but also during the year we will go on tour. Recent summer tours include Liverpool, Lincolnshire and Surrey
We do not practice religion. Even though you are situated within the confines of christianity, you do not need to pledge alliance to the cause.
